## FAQ Section

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is an AI workflow?**
A: An AI workflow is a pre-configured sequence of AI operations — like prompts, API calls, and data transformations — designed to accomplish a specific business task. Think of it as a recipe for AI automation.

**Q: Do I need to know how to code?**
A: No. Agentic Marketplace is designed for business operators. If you can use a web app, you can deploy AI workflows.

**Q: How does the marketplace work?**
A: AI creators publish workflows to the marketplace. Browse, review, and deploy them with one click. You pay only for what you use. Creators earn 70% of every sale.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, SOC 2 compliant infrastructure, and each tenant's data is fully isolated. Enterprise plans include SSO, audit logs, and custom RBAC.

**Q: Can I create and sell my own workflows?**
A: Yes! Sign up for a free account, build your workflow using our visual editor, and list it on the marketplace. You set the price, we handle the rest.

**Q: What AI models are supported?**
A: We support all major AI providers through OpenRouter — including OpenAI GPT-4, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, and 50+ more. You choose the model per workflow.

**Q: How are execution costs calculated?**
A: You pay per AI execution based on token usage. We show real-time cost estimates before each execution. Pro and Business plans include cost dashboards.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Downgrade to Free at any time. Your workflows stop executing but your data is preserved for 30 days.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. For marketplace purchases, our escrow system protects buyers — funds are held until you confirm the workflow works as described.

**Q: Is there an API?**
A: Yes. All plans include REST API access. Pro and above get higher rate limits and webhook support. Enterprise gets custom rate limits and dedicated API support.
